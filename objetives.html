The Company Drums&Boots

is a dance company that seeks, through the projection of Argentine folk dance, to tour different stages of the world. It is the product of the years of experience of 2 professional dancers who in 2022 decided to present their own work. The material they present is fresh and powerful, highly adaptable to scenarios, stage times and audiences. With the possibility of adding dancers.
